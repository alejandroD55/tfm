MГүTRICAS CALCULADAS:
====================

Sentimiento:
  вҖў Sentimiento: bullish, bearish, neutral
  вҖў Confidence: 0.0 - 1.0
  вҖў JustificaciГіn: texto

Indicadores TГ©cnicos:
  вҖў RSI 14:        0 - 100 (oversold < 30, overbought > 70)
  вҖў SMA 20:        Precio medio 20 perГӯodos
  вҖў SMA 50:        Precio medio 50 perГӯodos
  вҖў BB Upper:      Banda superior Bollinger
  вҖў BB Middle:     Banda media Bollinger
  вҖў BB Lower:      Banda inferior Bollinger

SeГұales de Trading:
  вҖў Signal:        BUY, SELL, HOLD
  вҖў Prob_up:       P(MarketDirection=up)
  вҖў Prob_down:     P(MarketDirection=down)

Backtesting:
  вҖў Cumulative Return:      Retorno acumulado (%)
  вҖў Sharpe Ratio:           Retorno ajustado por riesgo
  вҖў Max Drawdown:           Peor caГӯda capital (%)
